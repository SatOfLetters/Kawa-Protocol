Introducing Kawa

Kawa is a permissionless borrowing and lending protocol, streaming liquidity through the Sui Network.

Instant Liquidity

Maximize your Yield by getting instant liquidity upon your crypto assets and put them to work on a variety of different options.

Cross-Chain Collateral

Cross-Chain Collateral. Lend or borrow against your collateralized crypto assets from different blockchain networks.

Censorship Resistant

Kawa is a trustless, full decentralized and highly secure protocol which is controlled by nobody.
